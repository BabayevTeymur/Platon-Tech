ABOUT

ALS (Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis) is a neurological disease that leads to progressive impairment of executive function, social disappointment and ultimately death. Approximately 140,000 people worldwide are diagnosed with ALS during the year. We developed an AI (Artificial Intelligence) controlled wheelchair for people with ALS. Our solution to this problem is to enable disabled people to comfortably drive a wheelchair and, in addition, to help them communicate with the other party in any form.

In addition, the computer in the wheelchair is controlled by the head on the screen, which helps us to communicate with people. With this system, disabled people will communicate with people in both voice and written format. With the apparatus developed by us, disabled people will be able to move easily to any area without any additional means. In addition, self-isolation will be eliminated through the communication system.
